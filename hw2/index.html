RAMEN SHOP

GOAL
Enjoy one of the best authentic Japanese Ramen here at Ramen Shop! There are many who have eaten ramen but not many have eaten our ramen. Our mission is to spread more of Japanese ramen culture in the medford area. Additionally, college students may get tired of eating dorm foods and that is where we help out by providing our ramen.
STYLE
Ramen shop treasures the "natural flavor of the ingredients". We do not rely on chemically made flavors such as MSGs to make our bowl. Everything is made from scratch, including the ramen noodles and the soup broth. All the ingredients used are organic and thus is safe for people of all ages and genders.
